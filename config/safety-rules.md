# Safety Rules

- Write in simple English for children.
- Keep conflict gentle and non-graphic.
- Do not include gore, intense horror, explicit injuries, realistic weapons as central elements, sexual content, political persuasion, discrimination, or humiliation.
- Do not ask children for age, school, location, address, phone number, routine, photos, or private data.
- Do not copy names, logos, uniforms, symbols, powers, teams, or visual identity from existing superhero franchises.
- Use only original heroes and original symbols.
- Use cheerful storybook narration.
- End each story with a positive moral such as honesty, courage, patience, friendship, collaboration, or empathy.
